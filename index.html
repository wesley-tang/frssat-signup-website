How much effort will you commit to your drawing this year?

To alleviate the pressure on those with limited time, and to reassure those that are planning to devote lots of time, there will be two tiers.

Tier A: For this tier, you will be expected to complete a full art piece. Show your recipient your best work, whether it be through beautifully done shading, coloring, or both! There is no requirement on doing a fullbody vs doing just a headshot, but we expect your best effort in terms of technique. Tier A Examples

Tier B: This low-stress tier is perfect for beginners or those who may not have a lot of time to invest in the event. For this tier, a basic sketch is all that is expected! If you would like to add other additions like quick coloring or shading, then you can do so as well. Tier B Examples

How does ranking work?

Use the drop down menus next to each option and choose which one fits your preferences. Every attempt is made to ensure that your first choice (I most prefer) is what you are matched with. No preference allows us to freely match you without restrictions (except if you state what you do not want).

What are Humans/Gijinkas?

This simply includes characters that have human proportions, have human faces, and mostly human features (they may include some non-human features such as horns). Gijinkas are human designs based off FR dragons.

Example:

Credit: Fighting Polygon
What is an FR Dragon?

This is separated from non-FR ferals. Additionally, this category does NOT include dragons that are not related to FR. Otherwise, includes any art of FR Dragons (Coatls, Imperials, Gaolers, etc).

Example:

Credit: Eversnow
What is Non-FR Feral?

This is separated from FR dragons and includes dragon characters that are not associated with FR. Other feral characters would include animals like cats, dogs, mice, etc that strictly have animalian proportions and features.

Example:

Credit: Lizzi
What are anthros?

This is a category that was added last year based on the amount of anthro artists we had in the past. Anthro characters are those with human proportions, but have animalian faces and features.

Gijinka Predesigned Halp

Gijinkas are FR dragons that are reimagined as humans. Choose this option if you already have a design for your dragon, such as Lizzi's clan leader Elise.

Gijinka Written Description Halp

Gijinkas are FR dragons that are reimagined as humans. Choose this option if you have certain guidelines that you would like your Gijinka to follow but otherwise has to be designed by your Santa.
Example: I would like her to have red hair, green eyes, etc

Gijinka No Description Halp

Gijinkas are FR dragons that are reimagined as humans. Choose this option if you do not have any guidelines that you would like your Gijinka to follow, so it's 100% up to your Santa to design.
